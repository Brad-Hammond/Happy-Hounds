About Us

At Happy Hounds, we are passionate about providing top-quality dog walking services across the United Kingdom. With a team of dedicated and experienced dog lovers, we strive to offer the best care and attention to your furry companions, ensuring they receive the exercise, socialization, and love they deserve.

What do we offer?

All day dog walking

We offer an all day dog walking service which starts from 5am all the way to 10pm, Monday to Sunday.

Solo and group walks

We offer a one to one solo dog walk opotion as well as group walk with other dogs in your area! All other dogs in the group walk are posted in our chat before it starts.

Pet transportation

We offer transportation services to and from vet appointments, grooming appointments, or other destinations, ensuring pets get where they need to go safely and comfortably.

Senior dog care

Gentle walks and visits tailored to the needs of older dogs, including shorter walks, bathroom breaks, and extra care and attention for any mobility or health issues they may have.
